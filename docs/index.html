Core Principles

🔐

Privacy by Default

End-to-end encryption for your personal vault. No plaintext ever leaves your device.

🏠

Local-First

Your data lives on your device. The server stores only encrypted blobs it cannot read.

🤖

Zero-Knowledge AI

On-device intelligence via Transformers.js — AI that can't phone home with your content.

🌐

Selective Sharing

Encrypted public links for what you choose to share, with revocable access.
